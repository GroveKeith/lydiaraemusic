About Lydia Rae

In the heart of the rolling hills of middle Tennessee, there is a teenage sensation who’s turning melodies into magic, Lydia Rae.

From the tender age of six, Lydia Rae found herself enchanted by the spotlight, serenading the crowd with a timeless classic, “Delta, Dawn.”Little did she know, that the stage would become her second home and music her true love. Lydia Rae isn’t just a musician; she is a storyteller and a melody weaver. Lydia Rae is a cross-genre maven. Influenced by the fearless country tunes of Lainey Wilson, the vibes of Taylor Swift, and the poetry of troubadour, Noah Kahan, she delivers her music with her vibrato magic and creating a vivid experience that will last long after the last encore.
